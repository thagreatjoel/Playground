What is this?

We use all types of electronics everyday but never really understand what’s inside.

Hardware Playground is a ysws program where you:

  • Open real devices and explore their PCBs
  • Build circuits from scratch
  • Experiment and see what happens
  • Rebuild parts of real electronics

This is about understanding hardware for real.

What you’ll do

  • Open devices and study circuit boards
  • Build simple circuits on breadboard
  • Figure out how components work
  • Rebuild and modify circuits
  • Document what you learn

How it works

find  understand  rebuild  experiment  explain

There are no levels. Start simple or go deep it’s up to you.

Build your way

  • Breadboard  best for learning (extra bonus)
  • PCB design  apply for grants to manufacture your board

Earn Diodes

You earn diodes based on:

  • Understanding (Through Journals)
  • Time spent

Track your time through Lapse (optional)

Better explanation = more rewards.

Rewards

Use Diodes to get:

  • Single Board Computer
  • DevBoards
  • 3D Printer

Or go further:

Apply for PCB grants

Build and ship your own hardware

Simple rules

  • Build real circuits
  • Explain your work
  • Don’t copy or fake
  • Don’t experiment with <12 volts
