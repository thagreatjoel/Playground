What is this?

Most people use electronics every day — phones, remotes, keyboards — but never really understand what’s inside.

Hardware Playground is a hands-on program where you:

  • open real devices and explore their PCBs
  • build circuits from scratch
  • experiment and see what happens
  • rebuild parts of real electronics

This is about understanding hardware for real, not just using it.

What you’ll do

  • Open devices and study circuit boards
  • Build simple circuits on breadboard
  • Figure out how components work
  • Rebuild and modify circuits
  • Document what you learn

How it works

find → understand → rebuild → experiment → explain

There are no levels. Start simple or go deep it’s up to you.

Build your way

  • Breadboard → best for learning (extra bonus)
  • PCB design → apply for grants to manufacture your board
