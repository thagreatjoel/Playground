Guide

This guide will help you get started with Hardware Playground step by step. You don’t need any experience.

Start with simple circuits or basic devices, understand how they work, rebuild small parts, and experiment.

We care more about your thinking, learning, and effort than just the final result. Take your time, make mistakes, and improve as you go! That’s how real understanding happens.

Start Here

1

Choose a Device

Find a device which has a PCB . If you don't have a device to open up, you can find a device PCB online or else you can use Starter projects

2

Schematics

  • Understand the trace and identify the components, create a PCB with your desired pcb desiging tool
  • You must show the schematic before submission, make changes to components or add new components or parts to make it cool
3

Design

  • Design the pcb with you ideas.
  • Route the trace,
  • Complete the design.
4

Journal ~

Journal about what you did, what you learned, and what happened. This is the most important part of your submission. You can also include your mistakes, Ai usage for journals are not applicable.

  • Conductor depends on the Quality of journal.
5

Submit ~

  • Submit your GitHub repo and your work progress, images, demo to the Workshop. And get it reviewed
6

Rewards

Convert your Silicons and Conductor to craft a Diode. Exchange those diodes with cool rewards
