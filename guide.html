Hardware Playground

Start here

You don’t need experience.

Just pick a simple device or a basic circuit and begin.

Good starting points:

  • mouse
  • remote
  • toy
  • LED + resistor circuit

Start Here

1

Choose a Device

Find a device which has a pcb . If you don't have a device to open up, you can find a device pcb online or else you can use Starter projects

2

Schematics ~8+

  • Understand the trace and identify the components, Create PCB with your personal pcb desiging tool
  • You must show the drawn schematic before submission
3

Design ~10-15+

  • Design the pcb with you ideas make changes to components or add new components or parts to make it cool
  • Route the trace desired pad and components
  • Complete the design.
4

Journal ~2-4+

A journal where you show what you did, what you learned, and what happened. This is the most important part of your submission. You can also include your mistakes Ai usage for journals are not applicable.

  • Diodes depends on the Quality of journal

    • 5

    Submit ~4-6+

      Submit your GitHub repo and your work progress, images, demo to the Workshop. And get it reviewed
